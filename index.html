matthew brehmer matthew brehmer matthew brehmer

I am a human-computer interaction (HCI) researcher specializing in data visualization. As a senior research staff member of Tableau Research in Seattle, I design, implement, and evaluate new interactive experiences for communicating and collaborating around data.

I build interactive visualization interfaces, write research papers, contribute posts to various blogs, and speak publicly about visualization.
